products

DJI air 3

The DJI Air 3 is a powerful dual-camera drone featuring a wide-angle and a 3x medium tele camera, offering stunning 4K/60fps HDR video and 48MP photos. With up to 46 minutes of flight time and omnidirectional obstacle sensing, it’s built for creative and safe aerial exploration.

DJI mini 4 pro.

The DJI Mini 4 Pro is a compact drone weighing under 249g, making it exempt from registration in many regions. Despite its small size, it boasts a 48MP 1/1.3-inch CMOS sensor capable of capturing 4K/60fps HDR video and 4K/100fps slow-motion footage.

DJI mini 3

The DJI Mini 3 is an ultra-lightweight drone weighing under 249g, exempting it from registration in many regions. It features a 1/1.3-inch CMOS sensor capable of capturing 48MP photos and 4K HDR video at 30fps, with a flight time of up to 38 minutes using the standard battery, extendable to 51 minutes with the Intelligent Flight Battery Plus.

Drone.DR

Drone.DR is a specialized drone care and repair service, offering expert maintenance, diagnostics, and support to keep your drone flight-ready. With a focus on safety and precision, Drone.DR ensures your aerial gear stays in top condition for every mission.
